Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dining with us

How can we help you?

How can I make a reservation?

+

Reservations can be made through our website's reservation form, by calling us at +1 (234) 567-8900, or via email at reservations@savoria.com. We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance, especially for weekend dining.

How far in advance can I make a reservation?

+

We accept reservations up to 60 days in advance. For special occasions or large parties, we encourage booking as early as possible to secure your preferred date and time.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or changes to your reservation. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may incur a fee of $25 per person. To cancel or modify your reservation, please call us directly.

Do you accept walk-ins?

+

While we primarily operate on a reservation basis, we do accommodate walk-in guests when space permits. We reserve a limited number of tables for walk-in guests each evening. For the best experience, we recommend making a reservation in advance.

Is there a dress code?

+

We suggest smart casual attire. While we don't enforce a strict dress code, we request that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, beach attire, or excessively casual clothing. Jackets are not required but are welcomed.

How long should I plan for my dining experience?

+

We recommend allowing approximately 2 hours for à la carte dining and 2.5-3 hours for our tasting menu experience. We believe in allowing guests to savor each course without feeling rushed.

Do you have a bar area for drinks before dinner?

+

Yes, we have an elegant bar area where guests can enjoy aperitifs before their meal. We recommend arriving 15-30 minutes before your reservation time if you'd like to start with a drink at the bar.

Is there parking available?

+

We offer valet parking service for $20. There are also several public parking garages within a two-block radius of the restaurant. We're easily accessible by public transportation, with the nearest subway station just a 2-minute walk away.

Do you offer wine pairings with your menu?

+

Yes, we offer expertly curated wine pairings to complement both our à la carte and tasting menus. Our sommelier selects wines that enhance the flavors of each dish. We offer standard pairings ($65) and premium pairings ($95) for our tasting menu.

Is the restaurant accessible for guests with mobility challenges?

+

Yes, our restaurant is fully accessible with ramp access at the entrance and accessible restrooms. Please let us know of any specific accommodations when making your reservation so we can ensure the most comfortable experience possible.

How often does your menu change?

+

Our menu evolves with the seasons, typically changing quarterly to showcase the finest seasonal ingredients. Certain signature dishes remain available year-round, but with seasonal adaptations. We also offer special menu items that change more frequently based on the chef's inspiration and market availability.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

+

Absolutely. We can accommodate most dietary restrictions and allergies with advance notice. Please inform us of your needs when making your reservation and remind your server when you arrive. Our culinary team is experienced in creating wonderful alternatives for guests with specific dietary requirements.

Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options?

+

Yes, we offer both vegetarian and vegan options on our regular menu. We can also create a dedicated vegetarian or vegan tasting menu with advance notice. Please inform us of your dietary preference when making your reservation.

Do you have a children's menu?

+

While we don't offer a dedicated children's menu, our kitchen is happy to prepare simplified versions of our dishes for younger guests. We welcome children over the age of 8 and encourage them to explore diverse flavors. Please let us know if you're dining with children when making your reservation.

Can I bring my own wine?

+

We do allow guests to bring their own wine with a corkage fee of $35 per bottle, limited to two bottles per table. We kindly request that the wine you bring is not already on our wine list. If you have a special bottle you'd like to enjoy with your meal, please call ahead to confirm.

Do you host private events?

+

Yes, we offer several private dining options for special events. The Gallery Room accommodates up to 20 guests, the Wine Cellar seats up to 12 guests, and full restaurant buyouts are available for up to 80 guests. Each space offers a unique ambiance for your event.

Can you create custom menus for private events?

+

Absolutely. Our events team works closely with you to create custom menus tailored to your event's needs and preferences. We offer pre-fixed menu options, family-style service, or custom tasting menus. We can also accommodate dietary restrictions and special requests.

How far in advance should I book a private event?

+

We recommend booking private events at least 4-6 weeks in advance, and even earlier for peak seasons (December, June, and September). For full restaurant buyouts, 2-3 months advance booking is recommended. Contact our events team at events@savoria.com for availability.

Do you offer off-site catering?

+

For select events, our culinary team can provide off-site catering services. These services are customized based on your event's needs and are available for events within a 25-mile radius. Please contact our events team at least 6 weeks in advance to discuss your requirements.

Can I bring my own cake for a celebration?

+

Yes, you may bring your own cake for special celebrations with a plating fee of $5 per person. Please inform us when making your reservation. Alternatively, our pastry chef can create a custom cake for your celebration with at least 72 hours notice. Custom cakes start at $65.

What is your late arrival policy?

+

We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the reserved time. If you're running late, please call us as soon as possible. For arrivals more than 15 minutes late, we'll do our best to accommodate you, but your table may be released or your dining experience may be abbreviated.

Is there a minimum spend requirement?

+

For regular dining, there is no minimum spend requirement. For private events, minimum spend requirements vary based on the day of the week, time of year, and the space reserved. Our events team will provide specific information during the booking process.

What forms of payment do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover). We also accept cash but do not accept personal checks. For private events, we may require a credit card to secure the reservation.

Is gratuity included in the bill?

+

For parties of 6 or more, a 20% gratuity is automatically added to the bill. For smaller parties, gratuity is left to the discretion of the guest. For private events, a 22% service charge is added to the final bill.

What is your photography policy?

+

Discreet photography of your table and food is permitted. However, we ask that guests refrain from using flash photography and tripods, as they can disrupt other diners' experiences. Professional photography requires prior approval from management.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to assist you with any additional questions or special requests.
